Asheville DSA

Democratic Socialists of America

Asheville DSA is the Western North Carolina chapter of the Democratic Socialists of America, a political, activist organization that seeks to empower working people and marginalized groups and build a more equitable society for all of us.

Learn More

Socialists have a rich tradition of carrying out scholarship and activism to defend poor and marginalized people throughout the world, a tradition which we need now more than ever.

Events

We believe that politics isn't just about elections. We work with like-minded groups and movements to advance our common goals. Come work with us for the good of all.

Join Us

DSA is a dues-paying membership organization, not a political party. All local events are open to the public and, whenever possible, child care will be available.
